# Business KPI Dashboard

A professional business analytics platform that displays key performance metrics, trends, and detailed order data in an intuitive, interactive interface.

## What It Does

This dashboard helps you understand your business at a glance:

- **See Your Key Metrics**: View revenue, number of orders, conversion rate, and average order value in clear, easy-to-read cards
- **Track Trends**: Visual charts show how your revenue and orders change over time, helping you spot patterns and opportunities
- **Explore Your Data**: Filter orders by date range, customer type (new vs. returning), and geographic region to focus on what matters
- **Drill Into Details**: Click on a chart to zoom into specific days or product categories; see exactly which orders drove those results
- **Export Data**: Download your order data as CSV for use in Excel, email, or other tools
- **Smart Presentation**: Numbers automatically resize to fit perfectly on any screen, from phones to large displays

## Key Features

### Dashboard Overview
- **Revenue Tracking**: See total revenue and how it compares to previous periods
- **Order Count**: Monitor sales volume with trend indicators
- **Conversion Rate**: Track what percentage of visitors become customers
- **Average Order Value**: Understand the typical purchase size
- **Revenue Trend Chart**: Line graph showing daily revenue patterns with the ability to click and filter by specific dates
- **Orders by Category**: Bar chart breaking down sales by product type
- **Traffic Source Breakdown**: Pie chart showing where customers come from
- **Full Order Table**: Browse all orders with search, sort, and pagination

### Filtering & Navigation
- **Time Period**: View data from the last 7, 30, or 90 days
- **Customer Segment**: Filter by all customers, new customers only, or returning customers
- **Geographic Region**: Focus on specific markets (North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific)
- **Click-to-Filter**: Click any chart element to instantly filter the order table
- **One-Click Clear**: Remove all active filters to see the full picture again

### Professional Design
- **Clean Layout**: Information is organized logically and is easy to scan
- **Dark & Light Modes**: Works in both light and dark themes based on user preference
- **Mobile Friendly**: Full functionality on phones, tablets, and desktops
- **Fast Loading**: Data appears instantly with smooth transitions
- **Professional Polish**: Proper formatting for numbers, dates, and status indicators

## How to Use

1. Install dependencies and start the server
2. Open the dashboard to see all your business metrics at a glance
3. Use the filters at the top to narrow down to specific time periods, customer types, or regions
4. Click on charts to drill down into specific days or categories
5. Scroll down to see the detailed order table
6. Export data to CSV when you need to share or analyze further
